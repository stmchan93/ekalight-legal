Ekalight Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Ekalight ("we", "us", the "App") helps you scan trading cards, see prices, and track your collection. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

Information we collect

Account information. When you create an account we collect your email address and display name. If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your email and name from that provider (with Sign in with Apple, you may choose to hide your email).

Card scans. When you scan a card, the App captures a photo of the card and sends it to our servers to identify the card and return pricing. Scan images are stored to operate and improve recognition. We may use scan images to improve and train our card-recognition models. They are private to your account.

Collection data. Cards you add, their grades/conditions/quantities, and any purchase/sale transactions you log.

Usage data. Basic analytics about how the App is used (screens viewed, features used, crashes) to improve the product.

How we use information

  • Identify scanned cards and show prices.
  • Maintain your account and your collection.
  • Operate, secure, and improve the App and recognition models.
  • Provide support.

Third parties we share data with

We use these service providers to run the App; they process data on our behalf:

  • Supabase — authentication and database (your account and collection data).
  • Google / Apple — sign-in, if you choose those options.
  • Google Cloud — hosting and storage of scan images.
  • Scrydex — card catalog and pricing lookups.
  • PostHog — product analytics.

We do not sell your personal information.

Data retention and deletion

You can delete your account at any time inside the App (Account → Delete Account). This permanently deletes your account, your collection, and your scan data, and removes your sign-in record. Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

You may also email us at team@ekalight.com to request deletion.

Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Security

We use industry-standard measures (encrypted transport/HTTPS, access controls) to protect your data. No method is 100% secure, but we work to safeguard your information.

Third-Party Content

The application may display card names, images, trademarks, and other content associated with third-party trading-card products. Such content and intellectual property remain the property of their respective owners.

Card images displayed through the application are supplied through third-party data services, including the Scrydex API. This application is independently developed and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or officially associated with The Pokémon Company, Nintendo, Creatures Inc., or Game Freak.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy; we will revise the "Last updated" date above.
